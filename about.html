About Me

My name is Kent Sellers. I enjoy all things math and data and take pride in the quality of my work. With my expertise in R, SQL, Tableau, PowerBI, and Excel, I am able to analyze complex data sets and present insights in a clear and comprehensive manner.

I have a specialized knowledge of workforce development. I've worked with many of the public labor market sources (e.g., Census Bureau, Bureau of Economic Analysis, Bureau of Labor Statistics) as well as several private sources (e.g., Lightcast, Chmura, DatabaseUSA, Help Wanted Online).

I hold a Master's degree in Mathematics and a Graduate Certificate in Applied Statistics from Purdue Univeristy Fort Wayne. I have applied my analytical skills to a variety of industries, including manufacturing, health care, government, technology, insurance, and education. However, my true passion lies in helping any organization make data-driven decisions that improve outcomes for their employees, customers, or stakeholders.

When I'm not working with data, I enjoy spending time with my family. My wife and I have two kids and one on the way. We love traveling together and going on adventures. I also love learning new things and trying out new hobbies. My recent favorite hobbies have been electric longboarding, learning HTML/CSS (like making this site), and 3-D printing.
